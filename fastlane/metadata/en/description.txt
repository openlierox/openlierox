OpenLieroX is an open-source real-time Worms-like game. It's like 2D Quake with worms and ninja ropes - an excessive, fast-paced multiplayer shooter where worms battle with a huge arsenal of weapons.

Features:
- Fast-paced real-time action
- Destructible terrain
- Huge variety of weapons and tools
- Ninja rope for swinging and climbing
- Single-player and multiplayer modes
- Online multiplayer support
- Cross-platform (Linux, Windows, macOS)
- Open source (zlib license)

Based on Jason Boettcher's Liero Xtreme, OpenLieroX is completely free and open source.
